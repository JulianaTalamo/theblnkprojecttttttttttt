Mission

Our mission was to create a simple, yet effective career resource for women. Everyone has unique experiences and preferences in their career path, and that path is different for everyone. Through local job postings, advice, and the best resources we could gather, we hope to guide women to those different paths. We want to inform and educate women on how to take the next step in their careers; no one deserves to be misinformed.

Your career path is like a watercolor painting, you always start from blank at one point. We want to inspire women to continue painting their best painting.
